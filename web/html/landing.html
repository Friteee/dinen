What is Dinen?

Dinen is mainly used for browsing through restaurants and ordering and buying food from them, online or as a menu at the restaurant itself. It is also a platform where restaurants can use as an online website for reservation, showing their menu and purchasing food even though the user is far away. It also serves the purpose of a custom made restaurant webpage which can be customize by its owner to suit their own preference. Dinen is a cheap and affordable version of a built in ordering system, where it is widely distributed and can be use by all restaurants. Its ultimate purpose of course is to hope that people can find food easily and buy them with minimum effort and maximum efficiency while restaurant owners will benefit from using this platform as well.
